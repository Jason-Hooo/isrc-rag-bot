師母盃為北區大專學生原住民族族語歌唱大賽，本屆第30屆主題為《 Nasi 聲 · 命 》，在北排灣語中，「Nasi」意指生命與氣息，是一切存在的根本，也是祖靈所賜的禮物。而本屆《Nasi 聲 •命》象徵以聲音延續生命，以歌聲喚醒文化。

Hello everyone! 👋

💡 What is the Shimu Cup?
The Shimu Cup is an Indigenous language singing competition for university students in northern Taiwan.This year marks the 30th Shimu Cup, with the theme “Nasi · Voice of Life.”
In Northern Paiwan, “Nasi” means life and breath—the essence of all existence and a sacred gift from the ancestral spirits.The theme “Nasi · Voice of Life” symbolizes continuing life through voice and awakening culture through song.
